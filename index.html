GrowShow

GrowShow is a revolutionary software tailored for fans of the iconic sandbox game Growtopia who want to take their gaming experience to the next level. Developed by the "Owner", GrowShow serves as the ultimate tool for creating and customizing your very own private Growtopia server. With its powerful features and unparalleled flexibility, it redefines what players and server administrators can do within the Growtopia universe.

GrowShow isn't just about recreating the standard experience; it's about pushing boundaries. The software comes loaded with a variety of never-seen-before features that enhance gameplay and server management, such as:

  • Lua scripting support - Allowing you to write custom features and addons for your GrowShow server.
  • Maximum performance & security - GrowShow comes with multi-threading support (including sub-servers), it is the only source that can achieve such awesome performance to even run on a toaster! GrowShow does not and never had any backdoors. There are no duplication glitches and no other game-breaking issues!
  • Everything is customizable - One of the best features we offer is that you can customize pretty much everything you can dream of and it's absolutely user friendly way to do so!
  • Active community - GrowShow community is constantly growing, people share their awesome creations such as scripts, worlds, it is very easy to get started with GrowShow!
  • Fresh source - Well if you're wondering is GrowShow based on some other source? The answer is NO! GrowShow has been created from scratch over the past few years!

And that's just a little part of what GrowShow offers!
